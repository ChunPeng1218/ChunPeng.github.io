Chun Peng

 

Hi, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal (the business school of Université de Montréal) and GERAD since June 2018, where I am working with Professor Erick Delage. Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology in May 2018, and studied Master in Management Science and Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2015. During my Ph.D., I visited the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal from Oct. 2016 to Nov. 2017.

Research Interests: Data-driven optimization under uncertainty using stochastic/robust optimization, and with applications in (a) sustainable urban transportation and logistics management (e.g., emergency medical service location, humanitarian logistics, last-mile delivery, shared mobility); (b) healthcare operations management (e.g., operating rooms planning, telemedicine scheduling).
