Yuxin Chen 陈昱鑫                                     

 

Yuxin Chen is a graduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and the Department of Statistics at Stanford University. His degrees include a B.E. in Microelectronics with High Dinstinction from Tsinghua University in 2008, and an M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin in 2010. He is currently supervised by Prof. Andrea Goldsmith and Prof. Yonina Eldar.

Yuxin has also worked with Prof.iJeff Andrews (Master supervisor), Prof. Sujay Sanghavi, and Prof. Sanjay Shakkottai at UT Austin.
